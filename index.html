How it works

A four-step process that keeps NGOs accountable to their donors.

01

NGO Gets Verified

Platform admin reviews credentials and verifies the NGO on-chain. Only verified NGOs can create campaigns.

02

Campaign Goes Live

NGO defines a goal, duration, and milestones with fund amounts. A smart contract is deployed for each campaign.

03

Donors Fund & Vote

Anyone can donate AVAX. When the NGO requests milestone release, donors vote — weighted by their contribution.

04

Funds Release Transparently

If 30% quorum is met and majority approves, funds are released. Otherwise, donors can claim refunds.

Three roles, one system

🔐

Platform Admin

The gatekeeper who verifies NGO credentials and maintains platform integrity.

  • Verify & revoke NGOs
  • Pause campaign creation
  • Transfer admin role (2-step)
  • Cancel pending transfers
🏢

NGO

Verified organizations that create milestone-based campaigns and execute on their promises.

  • Create campaigns with milestones
  • Start milestone voting
  • Release funds after vote passes
  • Track impact vs admin spend
💙

Donor

Contributors who fund campaigns and hold NGOs accountable through on-chain governance.

  • Donate AVAX to campaigns
  • Vote on milestone releases
  • Claim refunds if campaign fails
  • Monitor live vote progress
